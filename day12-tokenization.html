LLMs don't see letters or words — they see tokens (often subwords) mapped to integer IDs. That's why counts, costs, and context limits are all measured in tokens (rule of thumb: ~4 characters ≈ 1 token in English). This is a teaching approximation, not GPT's exact vocabulary.
