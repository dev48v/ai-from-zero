🔨 Spot it in the wild

    
No coding needed today — just train your eye. Use this checklist on any "AI" product you meet.

    
    
      
  1. 
        
    1
    Ask: did a human write the rules, or did it learn them?
    
        
    Hand-written if/else logic (a thermostat, a chess engine's opening book) = AI but not ML. Learned from examples = ML.
    
      
    2. 
      
  2. 
        
    2
    Ask: is there a neural network inside?
    
        
    Learns from data with a neural net (images, language, speech) = DL. Learns with simpler math (decision trees, linear models) = ML but not DL.
    
      
    3. 
      
  3. 
        
    3
    Place it on the map
    
        
    ChatGPT → DL (a transformer). Spam filter → usually ML. A flowchart bot → just AI. GenAI (images, text, audio) → a subset of DL.

DL  (net):     model = nn.Sequential(nn.Linear(...), nn.ReLU(), ...)
